Project: E-Commerce Revenue and Profitability Analysis Dashboard
Tools: Microsoft Excel

Built an interactive e-commerce analytics dashboard in Microsoft Excel to track key business metrics including sales, profit, quantity sold, number of orders, and profit margin.

Designed KPI cards with year-over-year (YoY) growth indicators to monitor business performance trends.

Performed category-wise profit analysis to identify high-performing product segments such as Technology, Office Supplies, and Furniture.

Created monthly sales and profit trend analysis to evaluate revenue patterns across the year.

Implemented geographic visualization to analyze sales distribution by state.

Developed insights on top-performing subcategories and category sales share using dynamic charts and pivot-based analysis.

Added interactive filters for year, region, and customer segment to enable flexible data exploration and decision-making.
